Saint Laurent

ince 1964, Yves Saint Laurent launched the female perfume named after the first English letter "Y" in its name, and the YSL brand has been associated with perfume since then. Yves Saint Laurent's original perfume philosophy clearly distinguishes consumers' unique personalities and lifestyles. It is a typical personalized perfume. The popular "YSL Opium" is a perfume launched by Yves Saint Laurent after traveling to China in 1977. He was deeply moved by Chinese culture. He was inspired by the shape of the snuff bottle, brewing a dark fragrance floating, rich and rich The oriental mysterious YSL Opium, which is the first time to break through the traditionally named perfume, has been a sensation worldwide since its launch, and it still ranks among the top perfumes in the world; YSL Kouros perfume adds a masculine and healthy atmosphere; YSL Paris perfume tells the fashion capital Paris

And the gentleness and romance of Paris women; the YSL Jazz men's perfume, which came out in 1988, lingers in the international city like jazz, showing freedom and optimism.
